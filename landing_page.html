Our Mission: To empower people to create, innovate and invent solutions that improve their quality of life and contribute to social and economic development.

Our Vision: To become a leading company in Africa that fosters a culture of innovation and invention, and supports the growth of local talent and businesses.

Our Services

  • Innovation Consulting: We help our clients identify opportunities, generate ideas, prototype and test solutions, and implement them in the market. We also assist in pitching solutions to investors and securing funding for their innovations.
  • Invention Support: We offer technical and financial assistance to inventors with promising ideas that solve real-world problems. We help protect intellectual property, commercialize inventions, and connect inventors with investors.
  • Innovation Training: We offer workshops and courses on various topics related to innovation and invention, such as design thinking, creativity, entrepreneurship, and intellectual property. We also provide mentorship and networking opportunities to help connect innovators.
